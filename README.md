# Stock\_RAG

\---

I want this chatbot can give a insight of the a company from different angles with minimum hallucination base on collected facts, induced value from the fact, and analyzed result from the inherent analytical tools from this app. 



with these induced insight this chatbot will be able to answer the following questions:

1. Is revenue weakness coming from demand destruction, pricing pressure, or a cyclical slowdown?

2\. Are margin changes driven by inflation, labor costs, capex, or business mix?

3\. Is the company under pressure because of macro conditions, company-specific execution, or industry competition?

4\. Which recent news is likely to have real long-term impact on fundamentals?

5\. Which sectors or industries benefit or suffer under the current economic regime?



## Ingestion

1\. business fundamentals from annule and quarterly report and derived financial indicators from these reports; 

2\. macro environment; 

3\. knowledge ingestion(financial and macro glossary)(so that the model understand it better);

4\. analysis, result of the analysis comparing peers in the industry;

5\. general news with special categories(US, international, technology, business);

6\. news of specific company/ stock;

\---later---

7\. Research paper/ books that relate economic and stock market

8\. aggregate the chatbot and a trained machine learning algorithms that can dive deep in the relation between the current economy and stock market to give a more robust suggestion and insight
